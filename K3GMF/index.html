Profil GMF Aeroasia

PT Garuda Maintenance Facility AeroAsia Tbk (GMF AeroAsia) is one of the largest aircraft Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) companies in Southeast Asia. Headquartered at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport, GMF AeroAsia provides comprehensive services including line maintenance, base maintenance, component services, and engineering solutions.

Guided by the spirit of #ReadyToServe, GMF AeroAsia is committed to delivering excellence, safety, and customer satisfaction through continuous innovation and world-class maintenance standards. Supported by certified experts and internationally recognized facilities, GMF AeroAsia continues to strengthen its position as a trusted global partner in aviation maintenance.

Why Choose GMF AeroAsia ?

  • Global Certifications — Approved by EASA, FAA, and DGCA, ensuring international quality and safety standards.
  • World-Class Facilities — Located at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport with state-of-the-art hangars and test centers.
  • Commitment to Safety & Quality — Continuous improvement driven by a strong safety culture and ISO-certified quality systems.

This profile video showcases PT GMF AeroAsia Tbk’s unwavering dedication to excellence in aviation maintenance. Through a glimpse of its world-class facilities located at Soekarno-Hatta International Airport, the video highlights how GMF AeroAsia combines advanced technology, skilled expertise, and a strong safety culture to deliver the highest standards of aircraft maintenance.

Penerapan K3

Landasan Hukum K3

Keselamatan dan Kesehatan Kerja (K3) penting untuk melindungi pekerja dari risiko kecelakaan dan penyakit kerja.

Lingkungan Kerja
